Spring 2017 overview

This season of BG&B will all be about finding and restoring balance.

Instead of our traditional structure, we’re going to have a series of mini-seasons, each at a different bar and themed around a classical element. The premiere will feature an appropriate Tarot reading by resident oracle Kate. The middle will pair two shorter games. The finale will offer an opportunity for reflection.

Four mini-seasons, four elements, four bars, four games each.
